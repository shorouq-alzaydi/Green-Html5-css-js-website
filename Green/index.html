Our Awesome Care Tips

Caring for Indoor Plants

1.Choose the right location.


2.Give your plants the right amount of water.


3.Keep your plants free of pests and dust.


4.Fertilize if needed.


5.Remove unhealthy growth.


Caring for Outdoor Plants

1.Make sure your plants are getting enough sunlight.


2.Water them on a regular basis.


3.Make sure your plants are getting plenty of food.


4.Keep weeds at bay.


5.Dead-head and prune your plants when necessary.
